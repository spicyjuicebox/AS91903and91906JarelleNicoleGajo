WHO ARE WE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

Hello! Here at Sancta Maria College, the tuckshop team are proud to present the SMC Tuckshop website. It has been designed to improve the tuckshop experience and the current selection of foods and drinks by hearing your helpful thoughts and opinions.

HOW CAN YOU HELP?

Students can provide their feedback, questions or suggestions on the 'Q&A / Suggestions' page. As soon as possible, our dedicated team will reply with the best answers or let you know about whether your suggestions are something we could consider!

If you have got something you would like to ask or suggest privately, head to the 'Contact' page and email either of our tuckshop team.
